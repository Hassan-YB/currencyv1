OUR COMPANY
Who Are We?
We’re a fintech company building a financial platform and infrastructure to bring joy and ease on money issues to people around the world.
A hero is someone who does something other people couldn’t do, don’t want to do, or don’t think could possibly be done.
It takes boldness, belief and brilliance to be a hero. As a company, that's exactly who we are on money issues. ‘Dinero’ [dee-ne-ro] is the Spanish word for money and combined with ‘Hero’ [hee-ro], you have our name - DineroHero.
Our Mission
Our primary goal is to bring joy and ease on money issues to people globally.

Our second aim is to help people understand climate change and personally contribute to greener globe through better money
decisions.

Our Story
DineroHero was born from a series of experiences that motivated our founder to solve his problems
and those his close family members had encountered with sending or receiving money.

It all started with a very painful money transfer experience. Sometime in August 2020 our founder,
Dominic, tried helping his younger sister make an international money transfer to Vietnam (a payment to
her hair extension supplier). He got the recipient's bank details and had to make the payment via SWIFT
transfer at a local bank. He chose to start with a test of 200 USD just to see how it goes before making
other bigger transfers.


To put it mildly, the experience was a nightmare for at least the following reasons:

Neither he nor the recipient could track where the money was after the transfer. The sending
bank couldn't provide any information on this.
There was the inconvenience of being regularly asked about the status of the transfer and not
having answers other than frequenting the bank at least 3 times in about a week with no positive
outcome.
It was only after a little more than a week that the sending bank informed Dominic that the
money was to be sent back due to some incorrect recipient details.
He was given the option to pay another 25 USD (or 1% whichever was higher) in transfer fee to
make a second attempt to transfer the money. But worst of all, when the money sent back finally
arrived in his account it was only about 150 USD. Why? According to the sending bank, the
intermediary bank deducted their fees! So why wasn't he told about such from the start? All said,
if he had decided to make a second attempt, the whole transaction of sending 200 USD would
have cost him 100 USD (25 USD first transfer fee + 25 USD second transfer fee + 50 USD top-up
to the returned 150 USD).
To think this was just for 200 USD…what would have happened if a much larger amount was
involved? Of course, he refused to do the second transfer. But it was right after going through this
painful experience that Dominic started looking for better ways to solve this. In a short while, that
led to the birth of DineroHero!

Despite that awful instance, there were pleasant situations when he’d help his sisters make
payments in US dollars to other countries or online and then they would pay in the local
currency equivalent (at an agreed and consistently discounted exchange rate that he calls 'hero
rate') into any account he specified (typically his Mum's or younger siblings').

After a careful examination of such situations described above and how he's personally
experienced subpar customer service in consumer finance for years, or quickly sent phone
airtime and money to his family via cryptocurrencies with minimal fees, he decided that instead
of being a sole hero on those money issues, he would build a platform to enable and connect
more money heroes while tackling other painful money issues people faced. Hence, the mission
became crystal clear - to bring joy and ease on money issues to people globally!
