USE CASES

See how you can use DineroHero to deal with money issues

  • 1

    • Quickly Send and Receive Money
    Locally and Globally
    You can send and receive money in minutes and even instantly to your family, friends, and other people locally and globally. They simply have to be verified DineroHero users for instant free transfers, otherwise have a bank account, mobile wallet or cash pickup location close to them in our expanding list of countries.
  • 2

    • Pay Safely by Cards
    Online and Offline
    You can create virtual debit cards to make payments online while (or without) waiting for a physical card. You can also use our in-app NFC contactless payment feature to pay offline with specific virtual cards that have the contactless payment enabled by design. You only need to set up your fingerprint or card PIN or in-app alert to confirm your payment by card.
  • 3

    • Spend As a Group
    with Friends
    You can create a spending group with your friends and invite them to join and contribute money for your planned event or get together. This way you all get to split the bills early and easily. You can also send payment reminders to a specific person, replace or add more people as you wish. Every member sees how the money is spent.
  • 4

    • Recharge Local Phone
    Numbers Globally
    No matter where you are, for as long as you have your DineroHero app, internet access, and enough money in your wallet, you can top up phone numbers in more than 150 countries for free. You can choose to pay from your balances in available crypto or national currencies.
